Important — sequence & verification required
Before depositing your physical documents, please verify ALL of the following:
  1. The documents are arranged in the correct order as shown in the sequence list below.
  2. The physical items exactly match the documents you scanned earlier (titles, copies, and number of pages).
  3. IDs and photos are placed on top of the packet as the first item.
  4. Enclose smaller items (photos, ID cards) in an envelope to avoid loss.
  5. If you skipped scanning some items earlier, inform the staff before leaving.
Required sequence (place in this order):

  2. Certified True Copy of Grade 11 (Form 138) — 1st & 2nd Semesters
  3. Certified True Copy of Grade 12 (Form 138) — 1st Semester
  4. Certified True Copy of Grade 12 School ID (Front and Back)
  5. Clear Photocopy of NSO or PSA Birth Certificate (whole page)
  6. Applicant's Voter's Certification issued by COMELEC (not earlier than 2022)
  7. Parent's Voter's Certification issued by COMELEC (if required)
