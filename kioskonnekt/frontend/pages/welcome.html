Welcome to KiosKonnekt

Welcome! KiosKonnekt is an AI-powered university admissions kiosk that guides you through the application process.

What to Expect:

  • Profile: Enter your personal information and academic background
  • Documents: Scan and upload required admission documents
  • Interview: Answer AI-generated questions in a conversational format
  • Summary: Review your application before submission

Tips:

  • Take your time - the process typically takes 10-15 minutes
  • Ensure good lighting for document scanning
  • Speak clearly during the interview
  • You can navigate back if needed

If you need assistance at any point, look for the help button in the top-right corner.
