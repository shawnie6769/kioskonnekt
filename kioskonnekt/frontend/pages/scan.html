Document Scanning Help

Step 2: Document Scanning — Capture your admission documents using the camera.

How to Scan:

  1. Select a document type from the list below
  2. Position your document within the camera frame
  3. Ensure the document is well-lit and flat
  4. Click "Capture" when ready
  5. Repeat for the remaining required documents in order

Document Requirements:

  • 1. PLV CAT 2026 Application Form
  • 2. Grade 11 Form 138 (Sem 1 and 2, 83%+ GWA)
  • 3. Grade 12 Form 138 (Sem 1, 83%+ GWA)
  • 4. Grade 12 School ID (Front and Back)
  • 5. NSO or PSA Birth Certificate (Whole-page copy)
  • 6. Applicant Voter's Certification (COMELEC, 2022+)
  • 7. Parent Voter's Certification (COMELEC, 2022+)
  • 8. Two Passport Size ID Pictures (white background)

Tips:

  • Hold documents steady while capturing
  • Avoid shadows and glare
  • Ensure all text is clearly visible
  • You can retake photos if needed

All documents are securely stored and will be reviewed by admissions staff.
